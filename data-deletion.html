Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Ibis Publisher stores all data locally on your Mac. No personal data is held on external servers. Deletion is entirely under your control.

Delete All Local Ibis Publisher Data

To remove all data stored by Ibis Publisher from your Mac, delete the following folder:

~/Library/Application Support/IbisPublisher/

This folder contains:

  • Your post queue database (queue.db) — includes captions, scheduled times, and your Facebook Page Access Token
  • Any exported JPEG files awaiting upload (exports/ folder)

To delete it, open Terminal and run:

rm -rf ~/Library/Application\ Support/IbisPublisher/

Remove the Lightroom Plugin

  1. Open Adobe Lightroom Classic
  2. Go to File > Plug-in Manager
  3. Select Ibis Publisher in the left panel
  4. Click Remove

Remove the Companion App

Stop the background service and remove it from login items:

  1. Open Terminal and run:
    launchctl unload ~/Library/LaunchAgents/com.ibispublisher.app.plist
  2. Then delete the plist:
    rm ~/Library/LaunchAgents/com.ibispublisher.app.plist
  3. Delete the application folder from wherever you installed it

Revoke Facebook Access

To remove Ibis Publisher's connection to your Facebook account and Page:

  1. Go to Facebook Settings > Apps and Websites
  2. Find your developer app (e.g. "Ibis Publisher 2") and click Remove

This revokes the Page Access Token and prevents any further API calls from your app credentials.

What Was Never Stored Remotely

  • Your photographs — only temporarily on your device during export, then deleted after upload
  • Personal information — never collected
  • Usage analytics — never tracked
  • Captions or post history — only stored locally in your queue database
