Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Ibis Publisher and Ibis Metadata ("Ibis", "we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use our applications, including our Adobe Lightroom Classic plugin, web tools, and any associated services.

1. Information We Process

When you use Ibis, the following data may be processed:

  • Photographs: Images you select for metadata generation are temporarily converted to low-resolution JPEG copies (max 1024px) and sent to a third-party AI service for analysis. Original files are never uploaded or modified.
  • API Keys: Your Anthropic API key is stored locally on your device within Adobe Lightroom's plugin preferences. It is transmitted only to Anthropic's API servers to authenticate requests. We never see, collect, or store your API key on any server.
  • User Inputs: Seed keywords, location text, and required words you enter are sent alongside the image to the AI service to generate metadata. These inputs are not stored after processing.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Ibis does not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, address)
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Cookies or browser fingerprints
  • Your original photographs or Lightroom catalog data
  • Account credentials for any service other than your self-provided API key

3. Third-Party Services

Ibis uses the Anthropic API (anthropic.com) to analyse images and generate metadata. When you use Ibis:

  • A temporary, resized copy of your selected image is sent to Anthropic's servers via their API.
  • Anthropic processes the image in accordance with their own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
  • API requests are made directly from your device to Anthropic. No data passes through Ibis-operated servers.

4. Data Storage

All data processed by Ibis remains on your local device. Plugin settings (API key, output limits) are stored in Adobe Lightroom's local plugin preferences. Temporary image files created during processing are deleted immediately after use. Ibis does not operate any servers or databases that store user data.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share any user data with third parties. The only external transmission is the image analysis request sent directly from your device to Anthropic's API, initiated by you.

6. Data Retention

Ibis does not retain any user data beyond the immediate processing session. Temporary files are deleted automatically. Plugin preferences persist locally on your device until you remove the plugin or clear them manually.

7. Children's Privacy

Ibis is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect or process data from children.

8. Security

All communication with the Anthropic API is conducted over HTTPS (TLS encryption). Your API key is stored locally in Lightroom's encrypted preferences. Since Ibis does not operate servers or collect data remotely, the primary security boundary is your own device.

9. Your Rights

Since Ibis does not collect or store personal data on any server, there is no user data for us to access, modify, export, or delete. All data remains under your control on your local device. If you wish to remove all Ibis data, simply uninstall the plugin from Adobe Lightroom's Plug-in Manager.

10. User Data Deletion

To delete all data associated with Ibis:

  • Open Adobe Lightroom Classic
  • Go to File > Plug-in Manager
  • Select Ibis Metadata and click Remove
  • This removes all locally stored preferences including your API key

No server-side data exists to delete, as Ibis does not store data remotely.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of Ibis after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
